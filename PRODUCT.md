# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Shop owners and managers of SS Doors who operate in a busy shop or office environment. They need to quickly record client details, input custom door/chaukhat measurements, compute costs, and print order sheets for the workshop or clients.

## Product Purpose
A desktop database and calculator application that replaces manual paper calculations. The app prevents calculation errors between different units and item types (doors and chaukhats), retains client and order history, and prints clean, professional physical order receipts.

## Brand Personality
Professional, reliable, and utility-driven. The interface should feel like a robust, high-performance tool where clarity, speed, and accuracy are paramount.

## Anti-references
- Overly decorated SaaS landing page templates (avoid cards with soft wide shadows paired with thin borders, oversized border-radii, and tiny tracked uppercase eyebrows/kickers above every section).
- Low-contrast text (avoid gray body text on tinted near-white backgrounds).
- Cluttered, multi-column layouts that distract from direct order calculations.

## Design Principles
- **Direct & Fast Entry**: The user should be able to input measurements, update rates, and calculate totals in a single, fluent flow with minimal mouse navigation.
- **Precision and Density**: Show calculations, subtotals, and units clearly in high-density tables that do not compromise readability.
- **Physical Readability**: Visual layouts (especially print views) must be clear, crisp, and high-contrast, designed to be read easily both on screen and on physical paper.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Keyboard-First Design**: Complete keyboard accessibility for data entry, utilizing logical tabbing, visible focus states, and direct form inputs.
- **WCAG AA Compliance**: High-contrast ratios for all body text, rates, labels, and computed values to ensure legibility under varying shop lighting.
